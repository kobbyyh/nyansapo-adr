About Us

NYANSAPO ADR Center is a private third party impartial entity who adopts Alternative Dispute Resolution methods enshrined in (ADR ACT 797, 2010) to members of the public to obtain a mutually acceptable resolution to all disputes, voluntarily submitted to its centre. The centre and its practitioner are both certified member of Ghana National Association of ADR practitioners (GNAAP), a recognised registered professional association in Ghana by Law. To point up, NYANSAPOR ADR centre (TEMA) in line principles of ADR guarantee its clients a voluntary non coerced procedure to aid all disputants, resolve cases and differences.

Our Areas of Focus:

  • Labour & Commercial Disputes
  • Marital & Domestic Disputes (Child Maintenance & Custody)
  • Land & Boundary Disputes
  • Administrative & Corporate Disputes
  • Property Settlement Disputes
  • Landlord & Tenant Disputes
